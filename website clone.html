ABOUT US

We "Shri Ram Steel" khandwa are a technology driven organization working under name and brand of "New Loha Mandi" steel store in khandwa. Established in 1960 by late shri Shadilalji Bharti we have been working in steel industry since. Shadilal Gurandittamal our flag ship firm is still continuing business along with Gurudatt Steel, Bharti metal industries, Allied engineering and Shri Ram Steel. We at Shri Ram Steel are committed to give best quality steel with accurate weights and reasonable prices to our each and every customer. Equipped with modern technology for handling processing and manufacturing of d ifferent types of steel goods our team is always ready to serve you in the best possible way Our dear customers we expect your continued patronage.
